The trick

The price dances. The floor climbs.

One law governs the vault: no action may lower the floor per token. That single constraint turns crashes and manias alike into fuel.

Below the floor

The vault buys and burns

Panic sellers hand WALLY to the vault at a discount, and the vault retires it for good. Fewer tokens over the same assets lifts the floor for everyone still holding.

Buy cheap → burn → floor rises
Above the floor
