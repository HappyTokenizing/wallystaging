The DAO

WALLY DAO,
in plain terms.

01What it is

WALLY DAO is the first community built for people in crypto who care about Real World Assets. It runs its own treasury onchain, made up of assets that $WALLY holders vote on together. That treasury funds the mission over time. The Foundation takes no fee and owns no part of it, and all of it stays visible onchain.

02WALLY Holder Benefits
  • Partner perks and community airdrops that recognize genuine participation
  • Propose and vote on treasury strategies, grants, partnerships, and more
  • Be first to tap into RWA news from our partners
  • Enjoy ecosystem perks across partnered platforms
03Grant program

The DAO funds builders of the onchain RWA economy: DeFi tools, liquidity initiatives, education, issuer support, and transparency tooling. Milestone-based, voted by the Herd.

04Participation

Learning, contributing and helping govern the community is recognition for genuine participation — not for depositing or buying in. The Herd values members who show up early and consistently.

Membership

Our members don't buy a seat.
They stand for something.

The companies above are builders of the RWA economy — and they choose to support an independent foundation. Not for placement, not for favors: because an industry with neutral ground, honest data and real education grows faster for everyone, themselves included.

That's the whole deal. When members support the mission, the mission strengthens the industry — and a stronger industry is the win they share in. Win-win, by design.

They fund open education

Member support keeps every primer, course and data explainer free — for the whole industry, not a paying few.
They back honest data

Our members underwrite independent research and reporting — and accept the numbers even when the numbers aren't flattering.
They show up for the work

Standards drafts, working groups, issuer guidance — the unglamorous rooms where the industry actually gets built. Our members are in them.
How we operate

Three rules.
No exceptions.

PRINCIPLE · 01
Neutrality

We don't pick winners. No member — however generous — shapes our findings, our rankings, or our voice. Support buys the industry a stronger foundation; it never buys an outcome.

PRINCIPLE · 02
Truth-seeking

We verify before we publish, source everything, and correct ourselves in public when we get it wrong. Correct information is the foundation the whole industry stands on.

PRINCIPLE · 03
Mission-only

Every dollar and every decision serves one purpose: bringing financial services to the people of the planet — saving, yield, credit and ownership, open to everyone. Trustworthy RWAs onchain are how it happens. We hold no other agenda.

Believe the industry deserves neutral ground?

Then we're already on the same side. Member companies join because the mission matters — the conversation starts there, and nowhere else.
